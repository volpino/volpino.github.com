About me...

Hi! I'm fox and this is my simple homepage.

I'm a young guy interested in computer stuff, science, maths, Open Source and the Rubik's cube.

I'm currently attending the second year of Computer Science at University of Trento and I'm working for the Scientific and Technological Research Centre of Bruno Kessler Foundation in the SoNet group as a developer and researcher (well, i'm trying to do that actually).

We're interested in social networks and social media, at the moment we're studying various aspects of Wikipedia.

I'm a GNU/Linux user and lover, I like Python and C programming and I like web based stuff a lot.

In my freetime I contribute to a lot of open source projects and release almost everything I write as free software.

At the moment I'm working as a random contributor for the GlobaLeaks project.

Some friends of mine consider me a fool just because I use Gentoo on my Macbook (yeah, I actually don't know why i bought a Macbook) but I think that there're a lot of worse things to do.

I'm interested in IT security too, even if i'm not so good. Together with same friends in March 2011 we took part in the "Cracca al Tesoro" competition in Milan where we gained the third place.

I love any kind of geek gadgets or t-shirts and I have a sort of a geeky clothes collection ;). Obviously I'm a great fan of "The Big Bang Theory".

In my spare time I like improving my skills with the Rubik's cube and other twisted puzzles (without great results though). This is my WCA profile.

I like playing HackGames such as SmashTheStack, HackThisSite and similar stuff.

My favourite kind of music are folk, ska, reggae and rock. I like to listen to it while I'm coding or cubing. This is my last.fm profile.

I'm a really low skilled homebrewer but I'm trying to produce better and better beer and mead.

I don't know if this is good and healthy but when I work I'm totally goal driven, I can stay in front of my laptop for many hours until I don't finish what I'm doing. That's totally bad practice if you have other arrangements!

If you want to know more you can see my projects or if you want to discuss something feel free to contact me!


Happy hacking!
